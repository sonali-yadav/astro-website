About Us

Indian Vedic Foundation is a non-profit organization established in 2008 to share traditional yajnas, spiritual wisdom, social welfare and the secrets of vedic science with the world. It is also registered under the Society Act 21, 1860, U.P., India.

We are trying to bring to light, the benefits of using the vedic mantras to lighten up your life and find solutions to your problems through the laws of astro-physics. We perform yajnas, mantras, etc for people who would want to heal themselves and their life through ancient Indian spiritualism.
