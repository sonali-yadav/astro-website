Cow Shelter (Gaushala)

Donation Invites for Cow Shelter (Gaushala)

A Cowpen (Gaushala) is situated in Kanpur at Itahara farm where the cows of Sahiwal are being reared under a protected environment. The aim of preservation of the cows is to give better shelter and save the reared variety of cow Sahiwal and give them a natural vedic atmosphere under the mission of Indian Vedic Foundation.
